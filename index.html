01 — Legacy Sling

Digital Design & Lifestyle Campaign

The Legacy Sling was designed to merge utility with lifestyle. The challenge was positioning a functional camera bag as a refined, everyday accessory without losing its rugged integrity.

  • Product positioning & audience refinement
  • Visual tone exploration
  • Lifestyle photography direction
  • Digital campaign layout & asset system

The final system balanced durability with elegance, creating a product presence that felt intentional, versatile, and timeless.

02 — Modern Heritage

Brand Identity & Visual System

A heritage-inspired fashion brand needed a contemporary identity system that honored tradition while feeling relevant to modern audiences.

  • Brand strategy & positioning
  • Identity system design
  • Pattern & typography exploration
  • Lookbook & campaign direction

The result was a refined visual language that balanced craftsmanship with contemporary clarity.
